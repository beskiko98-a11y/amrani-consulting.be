# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

PME owners and operational decision-makers in Brussels (5–100 employees). They are non-technical or lightly technical — they understand the problem but not the solution. They arrive via Google search ("freelance IT Bruxelles") or word of mouth, scan quickly for credibility signals, and decide within seconds whether to reach out. Their core anxiety: "will this person understand my context and actually deliver, or is it another overpriced headache?"

## Product Purpose

Amrani Consulting is the personal consultancy of Abdelkrim Amrani — IT freelance and AI consultant based in Brussels. The site exists to convert first-time visitors into leads (contact form) by building trust before the first call. Success looks like: a PME owner who landed via search leaves with enough confidence to fill out the form without needing to do further research. Secondary purpose: showcase demos (ImmoAI, portfolio) that prove capability rather than just claiming it.

## Brand Personality

Clarté · Structure · Impact

Voice is calm and precise — like the colleague who explains something complex in two sentences without dumbing it down. Never hype, never jargon. In French, with occasional technical English terms where they are genuinely clearer.

## Anti-references

- **Fiverr / Malt-style profiles**: transactional grids, star ratings, "starting from €X/hr" energy — reads budget-tier and commodity
- **Generic IT agency sites**: stock photos of people in meetings, blue-gradient hero, buzzword soup ("end-to-end digital transformation")
- Any aesthetic that signals "this was made from a template"

## Design Principles

1. **Quiet authority** — Confidence is conveyed through precision and restraint, not bold claims or loud color. The gold accent earns its use; it does not decorate everything.
2. **Jargon-free clarity** — Every section should be understood by a PME owner with no IT background. If a concept needs explaining, the design should carry that explanation visually.
3. **Show, don't sell** — Capability is demonstrated through actual demos and case-specifics (ImmoAI, portfolio work), not through superlatives in headlines.
4. **Local grounding** — Brussels is a feature: the site speaks to local realities, not generic EU or global positioning.
5. **One clear next step** — Every page points toward contact. No dead ends, no ambiguity about what the visitor should do next.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG AA minimum. Body text ≥ 4.5:1 contrast against its background. Gold on ivory requires the darker token (`--color-gold-text: #7C5A1E`, measured at 5.17:1) — never use `--color-gold` (#B08D57) for text on cream. Reduced-motion alternatives for all animations. Site is in French (lang="fr"); no multilingual requirement at launch.
